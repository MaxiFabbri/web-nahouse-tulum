Location

We are located in Heaven Tulum, near La Veleta, a residential area. We are about 9800 feets from the beach area and same distance from main town in a different direction. We are surrounded by chic cafes, international restaurants, and casual seafood places. Is an up-and-coming neighborhood with easy access to Cenote Cristal, Cenote Escondido, and Kaan Loom Lagunes 3 turquoise, fish-filled swimming lakes nestled in the forest. Abou 15 minutes by car from the arqueological site in Tulum and 40 minutes from Tulum airport.
